Our Team

Who We Are

Rigel Software Inc. is a Vancouver-based software consultancy specializing in custom digital solutions. Founded in 2020, we bring over 25 years of combined experience in software engineering, UX design, DevOps, and digital strategy.

Our small team is highly adaptable, with a proven ability to solve complex problems, integrate into cross-functional teams, and consistently deliver exceptional results — on time and under budget.

What We Do

We help clients across Canada build and maintain robust, user-friendly systems — from full-stack web and mobile applications to scalable cloud infrastructure and useful tools that drive real value.

We work across the stack, across platforms, and across domains. Whether it's modernizing legacy infrastructure, automating workflows, or launching a new product, we specialize in listening carefully, moving quickly, and building solutions that last.
