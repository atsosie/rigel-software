Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 27, 2020

Last Updated: July 19, 2025

Rigel Software Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. We designed our website to be simple, accessible, and privacy-friendly.

We only collect personal information through direct client relationships, such as during contract negotiation, private correspondence, or project delivery. This data is handled in accordance with applicable Canadian privacy laws (e.g., PIPEDA), stored securely, and never sold or shared without consent.

If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us at: info@rigel-software.com.

Information We Do Not Collect

When you visit our site:

  • We do not collect or store your personal information.
  • We do not use cookies, local storage, or other browser-based tracking.
  • We do not run any analytics services (such as Google Analytics) or third-party tracking pixels.
  • We do not have forms, ecommerce functions, or account registrations.

Information Processed in Your Browser

Our website uses certain CSS media features to adapt the site's appearance to your device and accessibility preferences:

  • prefers-reduced-motion: detects if you have requested reduced animations.
  • prefers-color-scheme: detects if your system is set to light mode or dark mode.

These settings are processed locally in your browser. We do not log, store, or transmit this information to our servers or any third party.

Fonts and Site Hosting

All fonts used on our site are downloaded from Google Fonts and hosted locally on our own server. No connection to Google Fonts is made when you visit our site, and no data is sent to Google.

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub, Inc. When you visit our site, GitHub may collect and store standard web server logs (such as your IP address and browser type) for operational and security purposes. We do not have access to or control over this information.

Third-Party Services

We do not embed, load, or request resources from any third-party domains when you browse our site.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of the page.
